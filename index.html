About:

With 30 years of experience in fine art, graphic design, content creation, sound design, and audio production, I focus on functional minimalism, using color and composition as key design tools.

I prioritize understanding the 'why' in design, leveraging design thinking and user experience principles to ensure each project fits its domain. My innovative approach aims to create distinctive and memorable designs, emphasizing user interface design, UX principles, and digital marketing strategies.

I believe in thoughtful design to solve problems and create meaningful connections. My holistic approach considers every aspect of the user journey and aligns with client and project objectives.

Combining experimental and forward-thinking methodologies, I use a hybrid of AI tools, human creativity, chance, and ideation. This blend allows me to push boundaries and iteratively find the best solutions, delivering exceptional results.
